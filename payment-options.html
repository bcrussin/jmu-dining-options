Payment Options

Meal Punches

Each worth one punch or swipe. It provides access to several locations, including E-Hall, D-Hall, and select locations that offer Duke Deals and equivalencies.

Dining Dollars

Dining Dollars can be used at any JMU Dining on-campus location, including vending machines and concessions.

Flex

FLEX is a declining balance account that connects you with the JMU campus community. Similar to a checking account, purchases are deducted automatically and the balance is displayed after each transaction.

Debit/Credit

Credit/debit and mobile payments allow for faster service and an enhanced customer experience.

Cash

Many JMU dining locations have gone cashless, but you may still pay with cash at a few select locations.

Gift Cards

Some franchise locations at JMU allow the use of gift cards.
